A short film about Parvati, a young Indian woman in her late 20s, who returns to her childhood home in a small village after years away. She discovers her grandmother's old diary hidden in the kitchen, which reveals a family secret about a lost temple in the nearby forest. With the help of her childhood friend Arjun, a local guide in his 30s, she ventures into the forest to find the temple. The story explores themes of heritage, identity, and the tension between modern life and tradition. The village is dusty and sun-baked, with crumbling colonial-era buildings and a vibrant market. The forest is dense, ancient, and filled with the sounds of birds and running water. The tone is warm but mysterious, shot in a cinematic realism style.
