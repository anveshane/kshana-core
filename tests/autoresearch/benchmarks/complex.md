A retired astronaut, a street musician, and a marine biologist meet by chance at a harbor festival. The astronaut struggles with life after space. The musician plays a song that triggers a shared memory from childhood — they all grew up in the same town but never knew each other. The biologist discovers a rare bioluminescent jellyfish in the harbor that night. Together they watch it glow, each finding something they had lost: wonder, connection, and purpose. The festival fireworks reflect off the water as the jellyfish drifts away.
