# Character Profile: Jan

## Basic Information
- **Name:** Jan
- **Age:** 25
- **Occupation:** Blacksmith
- **Location:** Ashenmere village

## Physical Appearance
Jan is a young man of average height with a broad, muscular build developed from years at the forge. He has short-cropped dark brown hair, often dusted with soot, and steady hazel eyes. His hands are large and calloused, with small burn scars across his forearms. He typically wears a leather apron over a simple linen shirt with sleeves rolled to the elbows, sturdy wool trousers, and heavy boots.

## Personality
Jan is quiet, methodical, and deeply reliable. He approaches problems the way he approaches metalwork — with patience and precision. He is not given to grand speeches or impulsive decisions; instead, he observes, considers, and acts with deliberate care. Beneath his calm exterior lies a stubborn determination that borders on iron will. He cares deeply about the village and its people, though he expresses this through actions rather than words.

## Background
Jan inherited the forge from his grandfather, who was the village's master blacksmith before him. His parents died when he was young, and Maren, the village elder, helped raise him alongside his trade. He has never left Ashenmere and has no desire to — the forge and the village are his world.

## Role in Story
Jan is the protagonist who must confront the shadow demon threatening Ashenmere. His skill as a blacksmith becomes central to the conflict when he discovers that his grandfather's anvil contains a shard of light-metal capable of harming the demon.
