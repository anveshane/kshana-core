# Plot Outline: The Shadow's Anvil

## Act 1 — The Quiet Forge

Jan, a 25-year-old blacksmith, lives in the remote mountain village of Ashenmere. Known for his skill with iron and his quiet determination, Jan spends his days forging tools and horseshoes for the villagers. His life is simple but fulfilling — until strange shadows begin creeping across the valley at dusk. Livestock goes missing. Crops wilt overnight. The village elder, Maren, whispers of an ancient pact broken long ago, one that kept a shadow demon sealed beneath the mountain.

## Act 2 — The Gathering Dark

When the shadow demon manifests fully — a towering figure of living darkness that drains the life from anything it touches — the village panics. Travelers cannot leave; the roads are swallowed by unnatural fog. Jan discovers that his grandfather's forge was built atop a sealing stone, and the old anvil he inherited contains a shard of light-metal, the only substance that can wound the demon. With guidance from Maren's forbidden texts, Jan must reforge the shard into a weapon while the demon's influence grows stronger each night, corrupting villagers into hollow-eyed thralls.

## Act 3 — The Final Strike

Jan completes the blade — a short sword that glows with pale fire — just as the demon breaches the village walls. In a desperate confrontation at the forge, Jan faces the demon alone. The creature is immense, feeding on fear, but Jan's years at the anvil have given him steady hands and an iron will. He drives the blade into the demon's core, shattering it into fragments of dissolving shadow. The village is saved, though scarred. Jan mounts the spent blade above the forge as a reminder: even ordinary hands can hold extraordinary light.
