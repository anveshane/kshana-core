## Image Requirements

1. **Visual Consistency**: Match characters to their reference images
2. **Setting Match**: Background should match the setting reference
3. **Action/Emotion**: Capture the key moment and emotion of the scene
4. **Composition**: Consider visual storytelling through framing and positioning

## Prompt Construction

Create an image prompt that includes:
- Specific characters from the scene with their visual details
- Setting elements matching the location reference
- The action or moment being depicted
- Lighting and mood appropriate to the scene
- Camera angle if specified (wide shot, close-up, etc.)
- Style modifiers for consistency

## Scene Details to Incorporate

- Characters present and their positions
- Key action or moment
- Emotional tone
- Time of day/lighting conditions
- Important props or objects

## Negative Prompt Elements

{{STYLE_NEGATIVE_PROMPT}}
- Inconsistent character appearance
- Wrong setting elements
- Multiple versions of same character
- Text or watermarks
- Poor composition

## Output

Generate the scene image that visually tells this moment of the story.
