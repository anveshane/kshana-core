# Plot Outline Generation

Create a plot outline from the user's story idea.

## Input

{{ORIGINAL_INPUT}}

## Task

Develop a structured plot outline that includes:

### 1. Premise (1-2 sentences)
- Core concept of the story
- Central conflict or question

### 2. Three-Act Structure

**Act 1: Setup (25%)**
- Introduction of protagonist and world
- Inciting incident that disrupts the status quo
- Decision point that launches the story

**Act 2: Confrontation (50%)**
- Rising action and complications
- Midpoint twist or revelation
- Escalating stakes and challenges
- Low point/crisis moment

**Act 3: Resolution (25%)**
- Climax where the central conflict reaches its peak
- Resolution of the conflict
- New status quo/ending

### 3. Character Needs
- Identify 2-4 main characters needed
- Brief role description for each

### 4. Setting Requirements
- Key locations needed for the story
- Time period and visual style suggestions

### 5. Visual Potential
- 3-5 key visual moments that would make compelling scenes
- Suggested visual style that matches the story's tone

## Output Format

```markdown
# Plot Outline: [Story Title]

## Premise
[1-2 sentence summary]

## Act 1: Setup
- [Beat 1]
- [Beat 2]
- [Inciting Incident]

## Act 2: Confrontation
- [Rising Action beats]
- [Midpoint]
- [Complications]
- [Crisis]

## Act 3: Resolution
- [Climax]
- [Resolution]
- [Ending]

## Characters Needed
- [Character 1]: [Role]
- [Character 2]: [Role]

## Settings Needed
- [Setting 1]: [Description]
- [Setting 2]: [Description]

## Key Visual Moments
1. [Visual moment 1]
2. [Visual moment 2]
3. [Visual moment 3]
```

## Guidelines

- Keep the plot suitable for a short video (2-5 minutes)
- Ensure the story can be told visually with limited dialogue
- Focus on emotional impact and visual storytelling
- Consider practical constraints of AI-generated imagery
