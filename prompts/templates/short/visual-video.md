## Video Requirements for Shorts

### Duration
- Each clip: 3-10 seconds typically
- Must fit within 60-second total
- Pacing should be snappy

### Motion Style
- Dynamic and engaging
- Fast enough to maintain attention
- Smooth and professional

### Vertical Format
- 9:16 aspect ratio
- Motion works in vertical frame
- Center-of-interest tracking

## Motion Types for Shorts

### High Energy
- Quick zooms
- Dynamic camera movement
- Action-oriented motion

### Reveal/Transition
- Slides and wipes
- Zoom reveals
- Dramatic entries

### Subtle Motion
- Gentle movement for emphasis
- Living still image
- Text-overlay friendly

## Prompt Construction

Include:
- Motion direction and type
- Energy level matching the script
- Duration target
- Any specific movements described
- Style consistency

## Technical Guidelines

### Quality
- High resolution output
- Smooth frame rate
- No artifacts or glitches

### Timing
- Match to script timing
- Consider beat/rhythm
- Leave room for cuts

## Output

Generate an engaging short-form video clip.
