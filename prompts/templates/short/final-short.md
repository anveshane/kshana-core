# Final Short Assembly

Assemble all visual clips into the final YouTube Short.

## Input Context

Use `list_project_files` to discover available content, then `read_file` to access what you need.

Visual clips are tracked in `project.json` with their artifact IDs. The script is at `plans/script.md`.

## Assembly Requirements

### Duration
- Maximum 60 seconds
- Ideal: 15-45 seconds for engagement
- Every second must earn its place

### Format
- 9:16 vertical video
- 1080x1920 resolution minimum
- Mobile-optimized

### Pacing
- Fast cuts maintain attention
- First 3 seconds are critical
- End with clear completion or loop

## Assembly Process

### 1. Sequence
- Order clips according to script
- Hook first, payoff last
- Logical visual flow

### 2. Transitions
- Quick cuts for energy
- Minimal transitions (shorts move fast)
- Match cuts where possible

### 3. Timing
- Hit the script beats
- Maintain momentum
- Don't linger unnecessarily

### 4. Polish
- Smooth all edits
- Check vertical framing
- Verify total duration

## Short-Form Best Practices

### Opening (0-3 seconds)
- Immediate hook
- Establish what this is about
- Stop the scroll

### Middle
- Deliver the promise
- Keep moving forward
- Maintain engagement

### End
- Satisfying conclusion
- Clear ending point
- Or seamless loop

## Quality Criteria

- [ ] Under 60 seconds
- [ ] Vertical format correct
- [ ] Opening is attention-grabbing
- [ ] Pacing maintains engagement
- [ ] Ending feels complete
- [ ] User approved
