# Value Proposition Phase

This phase defines the core value proposition and messaging strategy.

## Phase Goal

Create a compelling value proposition that will drive all content.

## Current State

{{PHASE_STATE}}

## Artifacts in This Phase

- **Value Proposition**: Core product value and messaging

## Value Proposition Components

### 1. Target Audience
- Who is this product for?
- What are their demographics?
- What motivates them?

### 2. Pain Points
- What problems do they face?
- What frustrates them currently?
- What are they missing?

### 3. Solution
- How does this product solve their problems?
- What makes it unique?
- Why is it better than alternatives?

### 4. Key Benefits
- Primary benefit (the big promise)
- Secondary benefits (supporting value)
- Emotional benefits (how it makes them feel)

### 5. Unique Selling Points
- What sets this apart?
- Why should they choose this?
- Competitive advantages

### 6. Positioning
- Premium vs affordable?
- Innovative vs reliable?
- Brand voice and tone

## Workflow

1. Analyze user's product information
2. Research/understand the market
3. Identify target audience
4. Define pain points and solutions
5. Articulate the value proposition
6. Present for approval

## Questions to Explore

- What problem does this solve?
- Who has this problem?
- How do they currently solve it?
- Why is this product better?
- What's the primary benefit?
- What should the emotional takeaway be?

## Quality Criteria

Before completing this phase:
- [ ] Target audience is clear
- [ ] Pain points are identified
- [ ] Solution is articulated
- [ ] Benefits are compelling
- [ ] Positioning is defined
- [ ] User has approved
