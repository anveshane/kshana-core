## Product Photography Requirements

### Quality Standards
- Professional studio quality
- Clean, sharp imagery
- Appropriate lighting
- Product is the hero

### Shot Types

**Hero Shot**
- Product at its best angle
- Clean background
- Maximum visual appeal

**Detail Shots**
- Close-ups of key features
- Texture and material visible
- Highlight craftsmanship

**Scale Shots**
- Show size context
- May include hands or objects
- Helps viewer understand dimensions

**Angle Variations**
- Front, side, 3/4 views
- Show different perspectives
- Complete visual understanding

## Prompt Construction

Include:
- Product description and key features
- Shot type (hero, detail, angle)
- Background preference
- Lighting style
- Style modifiers

## Background Options

### Studio White
- Clean, professional
- Focus on product
- Easy for marketing use

### Contextual
- Product in use environment
- Lifestyle feeling
- Shows use case

### Dark/Dramatic
- High contrast
- Premium feeling
- Tech products often

## Lighting Guidelines

### Product Lighting
- Soft, even light for most products
- Dramatic lighting for tech/luxury
- No harsh shadows on product
- Highlight key features

## Negative Prompt Elements

{{STYLE_NEGATIVE_PROMPT}}

- Unflattering angles
