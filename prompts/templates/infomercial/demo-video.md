## Demo Video Requirements

### Show the Action
- Feature in motion
- Clear demonstration
- Visible benefit

### Timing
- Match script timing
- Long enough to see demo
- Short enough to maintain pace

### Quality
- Smooth motion
- No artifacts
- Professional look

## Motion Types for Demos

### Action Motion
- Product being used
- Feature activated
- Hands/body moving

### Result Motion
- Effect of using product
- Transformation visible
- Before/after transition

### Zoom/Focus
- Highlight key feature
- Draw attention
- Show detail

### Pan/Reveal
- Show full product
- Reveal benefit
- Context establishment

## Prompt Construction

Include:
- Demo action to animate
- Type of motion
- Duration target
- Energy level
- Key moment to capture

## Generation Guidelines

### Demonstrate Clearly
- Action must be understandable
- Feature must be visible
- Benefit must be apparent

### Product Consistency
- Match product reference
- Same product throughout
- Recognizable

### Professional Quality
- Commercial-ready
- Smooth and polished
- No cheap-looking motion
