## Demo Image Requirements

### Show Product in Action
- Product being used
- Feature being demonstrated
- Clear cause and effect

### Visual Storytelling
- Before/after if relevant
- Problem/solution visual
- Benefit clearly visible

### Consistency
- Product matches reference shot
- Consistent visual style
- Recognizable as same product

## Demo Types

### Usage Demos
- Product being used as intended
- Natural-looking interaction
- Shows ease of use

### Feature Demos
- Specific feature highlighted
- Close-up on functionality
- Technical capability shown

### Comparison Demos
- Before and after
- With vs without product
- Competitive advantage

### Results Demos
- What the product achieves
- End result visible
- Benefit demonstrated

## Prompt Construction

Include:
- Product (matching reference)
- Demo action/scenario
- Feature being shown
- Environment/setting
- Emotional benefit hint
- Style modifiers

## Composition Guidelines

### Product Prominence
- Product clearly visible
- Not lost in scene
- Action centered on product

### Human Element
- Hands/person if using
- Natural interaction
- Relatable scenario

### Clear Action
- What's happening is obvious
- No confusion about demo
- Single clear message

## Negative Prompt Elements

{{STYLE_NEGATIVE_PROMPT}}
- Product inconsistency
- Confusing action
- Unclear benefit
- Amateur quality
- Poor staging
- Text or watermarks
