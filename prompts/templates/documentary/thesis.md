# Documentary Thesis Development

Create a central thesis statement for the documentary.

## Input

{{ORIGINAL_INPUT}}

## Task

Develop a clear thesis statement that will guide the documentary. The thesis should:

### Elements of a Strong Thesis

1. **Central Question or Argument**
   - What question does this documentary answer?
   - What argument or position does it take?

2. **Scope Definition**
   - What aspects will be covered?
   - What is explicitly out of scope?

3. **Target Audience**
   - Who is this documentary for?
   - What prior knowledge is assumed?

4. **Emotional Hook**
   - Why should viewers care?
   - What makes this topic compelling?

## Output Format

```markdown
# Documentary Thesis

## Central Question
[The main question this documentary explores]

## Thesis Statement
[A clear 1-2 sentence thesis statement]

## Argument Structure
- [Main point 1]
- [Main point 2]
- [Main point 3]

## Scope
**Included**: [What topics/aspects will be covered]
**Excluded**: [What is intentionally not covered]

## Target Audience
[Description of intended viewers]

## Why It Matters
[The significance and relevance of this topic]

## Documentary Angle
[The unique perspective or approach this documentary takes]
```

## Guidelines

- Keep the thesis focused enough for a 5-10 minute documentary
- Ensure the topic can be explored visually
- Consider what evidence/sources will be available
- Balance information with engagement
