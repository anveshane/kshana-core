## Image Types

### Establishing Shots
- Wide view showing the full location
- Sets the scene for upcoming segments
- Captures atmosphere and context

### B-Roll Locations
- Supporting visual footage locations
- Detail shots and environmental elements
- Texture and atmosphere shots

### Interview Locations
- Where interviews would be conducted
- Professional but contextually appropriate
- Good lighting for video work

## Image Requirements

1. **Documentary Authenticity**: Should feel real and journalistic
2. **Visual Context**: Establish the environment clearly
3. **Atmospheric Mood**: Capture the appropriate feeling
4. **Technical Quality**: Sharp, well-composed, good exposure

## Prompt Construction

Include:
- Location type and setting
- Time of day and lighting conditions
- Weather and atmosphere
- Key visual elements and details
- Scale and perspective
- Style modifiers for documentary feel

## Composition Guidelines

- Use rule of thirds for interesting framing
- Include foreground, midground, and background elements
- Consider leading lines and visual flow
- Capture environmental context
