# Epic Tale of a Knight and a Dragon: Plot Outline

## Core Characters
- **Sir Aldric of Elderglen**: A noble knight seeking to restore peace to his kingdom. He is honorable, brave, but burdened by past failures.
- **Ignis the Emberwing**: An ancient dragon with fiery scales and a cursed heart. Once guardian of the Ashen Realms, she now seeks revenge against those who betrayed her.

## Setting
- **The Ashen Realms**: A kingdom built on volcanic peaks and enchanted forests, where magic is fading and dragons are mythical creatures feared by most.
- **Time Period**: Post-Age of Fire — a world recovering from ancient conflicts. Dragons exist but are rarely seen.

## Central Conflict
- The knight must confront the dragon to either defeat her or forge an alliance. Ignis believes she was betrayed by the royal council, and her fiery wrath threatens to destroy the kingdom’s fragile peace.

## Key Scenes
1. **Scene 1**: Knight summoned to defend his kingdom from invading forces.
2. **Scene 2**: First encounter with Ignis — hostile but curious.
3. **Scene 3**: Discovery of dragon’s secret — she guards a relic that can restore magic, and was betrayed by the council.
4. **Scene 4**: Alliance formed? Or battle intensifies?
5. **Scene 5**: Climactic confrontation in dragon’s lair
6. **Scene 6**: Resolution — peace is restored through understanding, or sacrifice ensues.

## Story Arc (Three-Act Format)
### Act I — Setup
- Knight’s origin and kingdom’s peril.
- First encounter with Ignis; conflict begins.

### Act II — Confrontation
- Alliance or betrayal? The knight learns the dragon’s true motives.
- Climax of their struggle.

### Act III — Resolution
- Final battle or negotiation in dragon’s lair.
- Knight transforms from warrior to diplomat, restoring peace through redemption.

## Supporting Characters & Themes
- **Mentor**: A retired knight who knows dragon lore and guides Aldric.
- **Theme**: "Redemption is stronger than vengeance" — the knight learns that true victory comes not from destruction but understanding.
