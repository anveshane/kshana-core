**Step 3: Outline Key Scenes**

—

**Step 4: Develop the Narrative Arc**
- *Action*: Structure plot using classic arc (Setup → Conflict → Climax → Resolution).

  - Climax: Performs in front of crowd — emotional vulnerability revealed.
  - Falling Action: Gains recognition but still seeks deeper meaning.
  - Resolution: Accepts that dancing is not about perfection, but connection — becomes a teacher to other bots.
- *Prerequisites*: Key scenes mapped.  
- *Expected Outcome*: A cohesive, emotionally resonant arc with thematic depth (e.g., self-expression vs. function, emotion vs. logic).

- *Prerequisites*: Narrative arc established.  
- *Expected Outcome*: The story carries emotional weight beyond plot — symbolic visuals and motifs will reinforce themes.
